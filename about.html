ABOUT THE BUILD

Where design meets precision

The Idea

This project started with a simple goal: create something small, functional, and completely custom. A coaster isn’t just something you put a drink on— it’s a surface, a design, and a statement.

3D Printing

The base is designed and printed using precise dimensions to ensure durability and balance. Every groove and edge is intentional—built to handle real use while keeping a clean look.

Cricut Design

Custom vinyl layers bring personality into the build. Whether it’s a name, logo, or pattern, this is where the coaster becomes unique.

Digital Integration

The website acts as the digital side of the project—showcasing designs, documenting the process, and presenting the final product in a clean, modern format.

Final Result

A fully custom-built coaster that combines physical design and digital presentation. Simple idea—executed with precision.
